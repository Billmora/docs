## Important Notes

- **Database Refresh:** The demo database is completely wiped and restored to its default state every 10 minutes. Any changes you make will be lost after the refresh.
- **Restricted Features:** For security reasons, certain features like outgoing emails and real server provisioning are disabled or simulated in the demo environment.
- **Plugins & Themes:** The demo includes a showcase of the default themes and built-in plugins available in Billmora.

If you encounter any issues or have questions while exploring the demo, feel free to join our [Discord Community](https://dsc.gg/billmora).
